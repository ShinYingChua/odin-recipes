Kimchi Fried Rice

Kimchi and rice is stir fried with smoky bacon and spicy kimchi juice. It's simply delicious!

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Kimchi
  • Bacon
  • SPAM Luncheon Meat

Steps

  1. Dice up some luncheon meat
  2. Add oil in a dryer pan and stir fry the kimchi. When the vegetables are half cooked add in the luncheon meat
  3. Add your cooked rice and stir
